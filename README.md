# Food-Delivery-Dashboard
Interactive Power BI dashboard for analyzing food delivery operations, including orders, revenue, delivery performance, and cancellations. Provides insights into customer behavior, peak ordering times, and operational efficiency to improve service quality and business performance.

## 📊 Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes food delivery operations, including orders, revenue, delivery performance, and customer trends. It provides insights to improve efficiency, reduce cancellations, and enhance customer experience.

## 🎯 Key Insights

. 📉   Revenue Performance: Total Orders & Revenue witnessed a significant	decline of -35% compared 	
					to the previous year.		
. 💰 Margin Analysis: Net Profit is currently under pressure due to escalating operational & high costs.
. ⚠️ Operational Alert: Cancellation Rate is critically high at 51% immediate process review required.
. 💳 Payment Insights: Card Transactions are experiencing a disproportionately higher rate of 
					cancellations.
. 🍕 Product Leaders: Pasta & Burger continue to be the top-performing  items in terms of volume.
.📍 Regional Focus: Chennai market is underperforming and requires strategic 	intervention to boost 
					sales.
